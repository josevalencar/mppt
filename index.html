Power Comparison

Key Findings

Maximum Power Transfer Theorem Confirmed

Both experiments show peak power occurs at approximately the same resistance range (~16-20Ω), confirming this is the optimal load resistance that matches the solar panels' internal resistance.

Maximum Power Transfer: R_load = R_internal

Experiment 1: Lower light intensity → Lower overall power output
Experiment 2: Higher light intensity → Higher overall power output

Despite different power levels, the optimal resistance remains consistent because it depends on the solar panels' internal characteristics, not the light intensity.
